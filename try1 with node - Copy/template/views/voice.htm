voice refers to you there is work done or work is done by the people or people is doing work. These are the two similar way to say same sentence in different form. Types of voiceThere are two types of voice active voice passive voice

There are two types of voice
active voice
passive voice
Active voice=== a voice is said to be in the active voice when it form shows the people or the things denoted by the subject does something.
Passive voice
---a verb is said to be in the passive voice when it form shows that something is done to the people or things denoted by the subject.

the subject of active voice is changed into object of passive voice and the object of active voice is changed into subject or passive voice
for example
Active he helps me.
Passive I am helped by him
Active Mohan beats Sohan.
Passive Sohan is beaten by Mohan.

the pronoun that are used in place of object in the active voice are converted into to subject into to following way
Her is convert into she
Us is changed into we
Him is change into he
then is change into they
me is change into I

"BY" is added before the object of passive voice
I am eating a mango .---a mango is being eaten by me.
He’s helping his mother ---His mother is being helped by him

the pronoun that comes after by in the passive voice are changed into following
for example
We are changed into US
he is change into him
she is change into her
they are changed into them
I is change into me

verb third form is always apply in voice.

To convert into active to passive voice we always use the helping verb of continuous tense.
Present simple tense
we use helping verb is/am/are.
Past simple simple tense we use helping verb was /were
Future simple tense we use helping verb will be/shall be
.For example
simple present tense
I help you--- you are helped by me
She teaches you ---you are taught by her
Example simple past tense
I ate mango ---a mango was eaten by me
he wrote the letter beautifully ---the latter was beautifully written
the poem opened the gate--- the gate was opened by the poem
.Example of future simple tense
She will be teach me ---I shall be torch by her

In continuous tense we use a Word “being” after the helping verb
For example
present continuous tance
I am eating a mango--- a mango is being eaten by me
they are discussing the matter--- the matter is being discussed by them
they are repairing the bridge ---the bridge is being repaired by them
Past continuous tense
the doctor was treating the patient-- the patient was being treated by doctor.
They were killing animal for nothing.--- Animals were being killed for nothing
Future continuous tense
They will be making a diagram ---A diagram will be being made by them.

perfect tense

we add “been” after helping verb.
